Leben in Deutschland

A multilingual learning app for preparing for the Leben in Deutschland test and the Einbürgerungstest in Germany.

Privacy Policy

Leben in Deutschland is designed to help users prepare for German citizenship and integration tests.

The app does not require an account and does not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or other directly identifying personal information.

Learning progress, saved questions, mistakes, and preferences are stored locally on the user’s device.

The app does not display third-party advertising and does not sell personal data.

This policy may be updated if the app’s functionality or data practices change.
